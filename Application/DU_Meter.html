DU Meter 7.30.4769

DU Meter is a program for counting real incoming and outgoing network traffic. Features: fully customizable traffic volume and modem connection time alerts; customizable daily, weekly and monthly statistics (both on traffic and connection time) with the ability to export data to Excel, Word, HTML, Text, and others; shutting down the computer when the traffic drops below a predetermined minimum. One of the features is the display of predicted traffic for the next week or month based on available data.
